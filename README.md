> **Slogan:** *transform financial outcomes and achieve financial excellence.*

## Overview
POG Advisory & Chartered Accountants is a forward‑thinking professional services firm serving clients across **Gauteng and Limpopo**, helping individuals, businesses, and public‑sector entities move from financial distress to financial excellence through reliable, tailored, and technology‑driven solutions.

## Vision
To be South Africa’s trusted partner in financial excellence—delivering innovative, ethical, and data‑driven solutions that enable clients to thrive with confidence.

## Mission
We empower clients with expert financial guidance, personalised advisory solutions, and strategic insights that support sustainable growth. Our work is grounded in professionalism, integrity, and measurable value.

## Core Values
- **Integrity** — Honesty, transparency, and ethical conduct in every engagement.
- **Professionalism & Excellence** — High‑quality, accurate, and reliable work supported by continuous learning.
- **Confidentiality** — Robust protection of client information and data security.
- **Compliance** — Rigorous adherence to applicable laws, regulations, and standards.
- **Innovation** — Modern technology and advanced methodologies for efficient, future‑ready solutions.
- **Client‑Centricity** — Tailored support aligned to client needs, challenges, and long‑term goals.

## Leadership
**Posani Gidja — Founder & Partner**  
Chartered Accountant (**CA(SA)**) and **Registered Tax Practitioner** with **10+ years’ experience** across the private and public sectors. Posani leads engagements that streamline operations, strengthen compliance, and deliver measurable financial outcomes.

## Services
- **Tax Planning & Preparation** — Up‑to‑date, compliant tax support that minimises liabilities and optimises outcomes for individuals and businesses.
- **Accounting & Bookkeeping** — End‑to‑end accounting solutions, including financial statements, payroll processing, general ledger maintenance, and periodic management reporting.
- **Financial Consulting** — Budgeting, forecasting, financial analysis, risk management, and strategic business planning.
- **Business Advisory** — Entity selection, business formation, mergers & acquisitions, restructuring, and growth strategy to enhance profitability and scale.
- **IFRS 9 & GRAP 104 Specialists** — End‑to‑end model development; **data cleansing and reconciliation** where ageing does not agree to the financial statements; and guidance on **writing off prescribed and long‑outstanding, uncollectable debtors**—strengthening compliance and reporting accuracy.
